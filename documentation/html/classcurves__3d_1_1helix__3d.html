Detailed Description

Circular helix around the Z axis.

The helix uses:

  • radius in the XY plane
  • pitch per full turn (increase in Z over \(2\pi\) in parameter)

Parametric form:

\[ x(t)=R\cos(t), \quad y(t)=R\sin(t), \quad z(t)=\frac{P}{2\pi}t \]

where \(P\) is the pitch per turn.

First derivative:

\[ x'(t)=-R\sin(t), \quad y'(t)=R\cos(t), \quad z'(t)=\frac{P}{2\pi} \]
