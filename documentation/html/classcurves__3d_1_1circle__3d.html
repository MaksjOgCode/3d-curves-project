Detailed Description

Circle in the XY plane centered at the origin.

Parametric form:

\[ x(t)=R\cos(t), \quad y(t)=R\sin(t), \quad z(t)=0 \]

First derivative:

\[ x'(t)=-R\sin(t), \quad y'(t)=R\cos(t), \quad z'(t)=0 \]
